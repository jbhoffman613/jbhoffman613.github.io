Gay is in. Slay.

I am a second year PhD student at The University of Texas at Austin advised by Joydeep Biswas and Swarat Chaudhuri. I am interested in neuro-symbolic program synthesis, robotics, and reinforcement learning.

I lead the NeuroSymbolic reading group, and I am the TA for CS 378: Safe and Ethical Artificial Intelligence. Additionally, I am a proud alum of Northeastern University Khoury College and CCIS.
